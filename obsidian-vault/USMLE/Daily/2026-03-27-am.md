# 3. Malignant Otitis Externa (MOE) 💀
### 🚪 Vignette Anchor
고령(Elderly) + **당뇨(Diabetes)** 혹은 면역저하자. 귀가 너무 아파서 잠을 못 잠 (**Night Pain**). 겉보기엔 그냥 외이도염 같지만 훨씬 심각함.
### 🚩 Red Flags (Must Know!)
- **Severe Deep Ear Pain:** 일반적인 진통제로 조절되지 않음.
- **Worse at Night:** ★ **Diagnostic Clue!** (밤에 뼈가 아픔).
- **Pain with Chewing:** TMJ(턱관절)와 외이도가 붙어있어서 씹을 때 아픔.
### 🩺 Pathophysiology & Findings
- **Nature:** 단순 감염이 아니라 **Osteomyelitis of the Skull Base** (두개저 골수염). 생명을 위협함(Life-threatening).
- **Pathogen:** **Pseudomonas aeruginosa (99%)**.
- **★ Key Exam Finding:** **Granulation Tissue** in the floor of the canal (외이도 바닥에 보이는 붉은 육아종).
### ⚠️ Complications
- 감염이 퍼져서 뇌신경(Cranial Nerves) 마비 유발.
- **CN 7 (Facial):** 안면 마비.
- **CN 9, 10, 11:** 삼킴 곤란 등.
### 🧪 Diagnosis & Workup
- **Initial Lab:** **ESR / CRP** Elevated (염증 수치 상승).
- **★ Gold Standard (Imaging):** **CT Scan of Temporal Bone** (Bone destruction 확인).
- (MRI도 가능하지만, 뼈 파괴를 보는 데는 CT가 유용).
### 💊 Management
- **Topical drops?** NO! 🙅‍♂️ 절대 안 됨. 뼈까지 약이 안 감.
- **Treatment:** **IV Systemic Antibiotics** (Anti-pseudomonal).
    - Ex: **IV Ciprofloxacin**. (뼈 침투력이 좋음).
    - 장기간 치료 필요 (수주~수개월).

## 🚀 Rapid Fire Recap (High Yield Summary)
1. **Scenario: Pregnant, large uterus, BP 150/90 at 12 weeks**
    - **Diagnosis:** Hydatidiform Mole (Molar Pregnancy)
    - **Key Action:** Transvaginal Ultrasound ("Snowstorm"), Suction D&C
2. **Post-treatment Surveillance for Mole**
    - **Rule:** Weekly β-hCG to zero → Monthly x 6 months.
    - **Must-Do:** Reliable Contraception (Pills, etc.) to distinguish rising β-hCG from new pregnancy vs. malignancy.
3. **Scenario: 6 months postpartum, hemoptysis (coughing blood), vaginal nodule**
    - **Diagnosis:** Choriocarcinoma
    - **Key Trap:** DO NOT Biopsy the vaginal mass (bleeding risk).
    - **Key Action:** Check β-hCG, Start Chemotherapy (Methotrexate).
4. **Hyperemesis Criteria**
    - **Facts:** >5% Weight loss + Ketonuria.
    - **Metabolic Lab:** Hypochloremic Metabolic Alkalosis + Hypokalemia.
5. **Safety First in Hyperemesis**
    - **Trap:** Giving D5W (Sugar water) before vitamins.
    - **Fact:** Thiamine BEFORE Glucose to prevent Wernicke's.
