# 2. Otitis Externa (Swimmer's Ear) 🏊‍♂️
### 🚪 Vignette Anchor
젊은 수영 선수 혹은 여름철 휴가를 다녀온 환자. 귀를 살짝 스치기만 해도 자지러지게 아파함 (Tragus tenderness).
### 🦠 Pathophysiology & Risk Factors
- **Water Exposure:** 물이 외이도(Ear canal)의 pH를 변화시킴 (Alkaline화).
- **Barrier Break:** 알칼리성 환경이 피부의 방어벽(Acidic defense barrier)을 무너뜨림 -> 세균 침투 용이.
- **Cotton Swabs (면봉):** "Micro-abrasions"을 유발하여 세균이 자라기 딱 좋은 환경 제공 (박테리아가 사랑함 ❤️).

2. **Staphylococcus aureus**

# 🚀 Rapid Fire Recap: Must-Know High Yield Summary
## 1. Aortic Regurgitation (AR)
- **Mechanism:** Volume Overload $\rightarrow$ Eccentric Hypertrophy.
- **Signs:** Water-hammer pulse, Widened Pulse Pressure.
- **Differentiation (Location):**
    - **Right** Sternal Border murmur $\rightarrow$ **Aortic Root** issue (Marfan).
    - **Left** Sternal Border murmur $\rightarrow$ **Valve Leaflet** issue (Endocarditis).
## 2. Mitral Regurgitation (MR)
- **The Trap (Acute MR):** **Normal/High LVEF** is a sign of severe hemodynamic collapse (Shock/Pulmonary Edema). Don't be fooled by the number.
- **Surgery Timing (Chronic MR):** Operate when **LVEF < 60%** (even if asymptomatic). EF < 60% means LV dysfunction represents significant damage.
- **Pathology:** MVP causes Mid-systolic click.
## 3. Right Sided Murmurs
- **Key Action:** Ask patient to breathe in (**Inspiration**).
- **Rule:** Inspiration increases venous return $\rightarrow$ **Right-sided murmurs get LOUDER**. (Left-sided murmurs do not).
## 4. Tricuspid Atresia
- **Clinical Picture:** Cyanotic Newborn.
- **Key ECG Finding:** **Left Axis Deviation (LAD)**. (Since normal newborns have RAD, LAD implies a hypoplastic RV due to atresia).
