    - **A:** **G6PD Deficiency**. (Antibiotic trigger).

    - **A:** **PNH (Paroxysmal Nocturnal Hemoglobinuria)**.
    - **A:** **Flow Cytometry** (Looking for CD55/59 absence).
- **Q: Sickle Cell patient with fever, chest pain, new infiltrate on CXR?**
    - **A:** **Acute Chest Syndrome**. (Treat aggressively!).
    Ceftriaxone, Azithromycin ★
- Q: SCD stroke prevention
    - A: 뇌 도플러 혈류 속도 검사.
- **Q: Sickle Cell patient with osteomyelitis. Most likely bug?**
    - **A:** **Salmonella**.
- Q: HS에서 혈역학적 소견

    - **A:** **Hereditary Spherocytosis**.
- **Q: Treatment for Hereditary Spherocytosis?**
