# 2. Otitis Externa (Swimmer's Ear) 🏊‍♂️
### 🚪 Vignette Anchor

### 🦠 Pathophysiology & Risk Factors
- **Water Exposure:** 물이 외이도(Ear canal)의 pH를 변화시킴 (Alkaline화).

# 7. Chronic Suppurative Otitis Media (CSOM) ⏳
### 🚪 Vignette Anchor
6주 이상 지속되는 귀 고름(Drainage). **고막 천공(Perforation)**이 보임. 통증은 없을 수도 있음.
### 🩺 Definition
- **Chronic Drainage (>6 weeks)** through a perforated TM.
- **Pathogens:** *Pseudomonas*, *S. aureus*, Anaerobes (Zoo 🦁 - Polymicrobial).
### 💊 Management
- **Oral Antibiotics?** **FAIL.** (흉터 조직 때문에 혈류 공급이 안 좋아서 약이 못 감).
- **★ Gold Standard:** **Topical Fluoroquinolone Drops** (e.g., Ofloxacin drops).
    - 구멍 난 고막을 통해 직접 중이로 약을 전달.
# 8. Cholesteatoma 🐚
### 🚪 Vignette Anchor
만성적인 귀 분비물. 고막 뒤에 **진주 같은 하얀 덩어리(Pearly white mass)**가 보임.
### 🩺 Pathophysiology
- **Not a Tumor:** 암(Cancer) 아님. **Keratinized Squamous Epithelium** (피부 조직)이 중이(Middle ear) 안으로 자라 들어간 것.
- **Location:** 주로 고막의 윗부분(Pars flaccida)의 **Retraction Pocket**에서 시작.
- **Danger:** **Erosive!** 효소(Enzymes)를 분비해서 주변 뼈(Ossicles, Mastoid, Inner ear)를 녹임. -> 영구적 청력 소실, 어지러움, 안면 마비 유발 가능.

# 7.0-7.5📋 Senior Medical Scribe & USMLE Expert: Lecture Notes
**Overview (TL;DR)**
Here is the high-yield summary of the "Newborn Sludge, Vampire Effect, and Milk Anemia" lecture:
- **Neonatal Polycythemia:** "Tomato Red" baby due to intrauterine hypoxia or delayed cord clamping. **Trap:** Capillary heel pricks overestimate Hct. **Action:** Confirm with Venous Draw.
- **Anemia of Prematurity:** Preemies have an "EPO gap" because the liver stops and kidneys haven't started. **Vampire Effect:** Iatrogenic blood loss from frequent labs worsens this. **Tx:** Limit blood draws.
- **Milk Anemia (IDA):** Toddlers drinking >24oz cow's milk. Triple threat: Low iron content, displacement of food, and micro-colitis (bleeding). **Dx:** Mentzer Index > 13.
- **Pancytopenia:** Bone marrow failure (Aplastic vs. Leukemia). Look at the Spleen size to differentiate production failure vs. destruction/sequestration.
