# 2. Otitis Externa (Swimmer's Ear) 🏊‍♂️
### 🚪 Vignette Anchor
젊은 수영 선수 혹은 여름철 휴가를 다녀온 환자. 귀를 살짝 스치기만 해도 자지러지게 아파함 (Tragus tenderness).

2. **Staphylococcus aureus**

# 3. Malignant Otitis Externa (MOE) 💀
### 🚪 Vignette Anchor
고령(Elderly) + **당뇨(Diabetes)** 혹은 면역저하자. 귀가 너무 아파서 잠을 못 잠 (**Night Pain**). 겉보기엔 그냥 외이도염 같지만 훨씬 심각함.
### 🚩 Red Flags (Must Know!)
- **Severe Deep Ear Pain:** 일반적인 진통제로 조절되지 않음.
- **Worse at Night:** ★ **Diagnostic Clue!** (밤에 뼈가 아픔).
- **Pain with Chewing:** TMJ(턱관절)와 외이도가 붙어있어서 씹을 때 아픔.
### 🩺 Pathophysiology & Findings
- **Nature:** 단순 감염이 아니라 **Osteomyelitis of the Skull Base** (두개저 골수염). 생명을 위협함(Life-threatening).
- **Pathogen:** **Pseudomonas aeruginosa (99%)**.
- **★ Key Exam Finding:** **Granulation Tissue** in the floor of the canal (외이도 바닥에 보이는 붉은 육아종).
### ⚠️ Complications
- 감염이 퍼져서 뇌신경(Cranial Nerves) 마비 유발.
- **CN 7 (Facial):** 안면 마비.
- **CN 9, 10, 11:** 삼킴 곤란 등.
### 🧪 Diagnosis & Workup
- **Initial Lab:** **ESR / CRP** Elevated (염증 수치 상승).
- **★ Gold Standard (Imaging):** **CT Scan of Temporal Bone** (Bone destruction 확인).
- (MRI도 가능하지만, 뼈 파괴를 보는 데는 CT가 유용).
### 💊 Management
- **Topical drops?** NO! 🙅‍♂️ 절대 안 됨. 뼈까지 약이 안 감.
- **Treatment:** **IV Systemic Antibiotics** (Anti-pseudomonal).
    - Ex: **IV Ciprofloxacin**. (뼈 침투력이 좋음).
    - 장기간 치료 필요 (수주~수개월).

# 7.0-7.5📋 Senior Medical Scribe & USMLE Expert: Lecture Notes
**Overview (TL;DR)**
Here is the high-yield summary of the "Newborn Sludge, Vampire Effect, and Milk Anemia" lecture:
- **Neonatal Polycythemia:** "Tomato Red" baby due to intrauterine hypoxia or delayed cord clamping. **Trap:** Capillary heel pricks overestimate Hct. **Action:** Confirm with Venous Draw.
- **Anemia of Prematurity:** Preemies have an "EPO gap" because the liver stops and kidneys haven't started. **Vampire Effect:** Iatrogenic blood loss from frequent labs worsens this. **Tx:** Limit blood draws.
- **Milk Anemia (IDA):** Toddlers drinking >24oz cow's milk. Triple threat: Low iron content, displacement of food, and micro-colitis (bleeding). **Dx:** Mentzer Index > 13.
- **Pancytopenia:** Bone marrow failure (Aplastic vs. Leukemia). Look at the Spleen size to differentiate production failure vs. destruction/sequestration.
